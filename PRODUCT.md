# Product

## Register

product

## Users
General privacy-sensitive professionals (including lawyers, researchers, and corporate office workers) who require local-first, zero-transit offline document and data operations in the browser.

## Product Purpose
Provide a high-fidelity, high-security utility suite that merges, splits, stamps (Bates), converts, and hashes documents without transferring data to external servers.

## Brand Personality
Sleek & Premium Dark Mode. Secure, clean, high-fidelity, with sharp cyan and amber visual feedback cues.

## Anti-references
SaaS gradient text slop, decorative side-stripe card borders, nested cards, overstimulating motion, and the cream/sand saturated background monoculture.

## Design Principles
- **Local-first trust**: Visual cues indicating complete client-side sandboxed execution.
- **Expert legibility**: High-contrast typography and semantic space rhythm.
- **Drag-and-drop clarity**: Direct visual feedback for document organization.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Full WCAG AA compliance, proper contrast for all textual data, layout adaptations for desktop and mobile, and prefers-reduced-motion safety.
